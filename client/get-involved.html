FIEN offers global opportunities for young professionals and educators to shape inclusive education across Tanzania.

Why Intern With FIEN?

Our interns play a direct role in transforming education—from building websites and teaching digital literacy to managing grants and supporting student well-being. We believe that youth-led innovation drives real, lasting change.

  • Make tangible impact: Create solutions in classrooms and communities.
  • Work cross-culturally: Collaborate with local leaders and global peers.
  • Grow professionally: Build real-world skills that matter—tech, teaching, research, and more.

Web Development Technician

  • Build responsive websites for FIEN partner schools.
  • Collaborate on content, design, and updates.
  • Ensure accessibility, SEO, and strong UX.

Computer Technician

  • Support hardware, software, and local networks.
  • Maintain security, backups, and system uptime.
  • Train school staff on IT maintenance.

Communications & Grant Manager

  • Write grant proposals and newsletters.
  • Lead social media and donor communication.
  • Craft compelling storytelling and visual assets.

ICS Teacher

  • Deliver tech classes using project-based learning.
  • Teach programming, digital tools, and safety.
  • Mentor students in coding and creative thinking.

English Language Teacher

  • Teach grammar, reading, writing, and speaking.
  • Use debates, games, and storytelling.
  • Improve student fluency and confidence.

Mathematics Teacher

  • Design lessons that connect math to real life.
  • Incorporate digital tools and student projects.
  • Foster collaboration and conceptual thinking.
